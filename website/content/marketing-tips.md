# Creator Marketing Tips

An Ophinia moment is more than a signature. It is an authenticated record tied to your name, your image, and your reputation. How you build your catalog matters. Here's how to make your moments easier for fans to discover, share, and order as official prints.

---

## 1. Start Small and Stay Consistent

Your first series doesn't need to be your biggest. Start with a small, focused release — even five to ten moments — and complete it before launching another.

Collectors pay attention to creators who follow through. A complete series with prints available is easier for fans to understand, share, and revisit than a large series that stalls halfway.

---

## 2. Use Series to Tell a Story

Every moment you create can be part of a named series. Series help fans understand how your catalog is organized and why each release matters.

- A small series can feel personal and focused
- A larger series can feel like a full release
- An open-ended series can grow around a season, tour, event, or theme

Think about the story you want fans to follow before you decide how to structure a series.

---

## 3. Write a Series Name That Means Something

Your series name can appear on Certificates of Authenticity and Official Prints. Choose names that reflect a moment, a milestone, or a theme — not just a number.

Examples:
- "Opening Night" for your first release
- "Season 1" if you plan to release in chapters
- A city or event name if it was captured somewhere meaningful

Fans remember names. Make yours worth remembering.

---

## 4. Enable Prints on Your Best Work

Official Prints are the primary way fans can own something physical from your catalog. When you enable prints on a moment, fans can order an official print directly through Ophinia.

Before you can enable prints, you'll need to complete payout setup in your Account. This connects your bank account so you can receive creator earnings from print sales.

A few things to keep in mind:
- Enable prints on moments you're proud of — print quality reflects on you
- A focused series with prints enabled is easier for fans to browse and share
- Official prints are connected to Ophinia's authenticity records

Not every moment needs to have prints available. Being selective makes the ones that do feel more intentional.

---

## 5. Make Each Moment Easy to Share

The easier it is for fans to find your profile, the easier it is for them to order official prints. Treat every public link, QR code, and social post as a path back to your catalog.

A few ways to help:
- Share your profile link after every new release
- Point fans to the specific moment you want them to print
- Use clear captions that explain why the moment matters
- Mention that official prints are available directly through Ophinia

---

## 6. Share Your Profile QR Code Everywhere

Your Ophinia profile QR code links directly to your profile. Put it to work:

- Add it to your social media bio
- Print it on business cards or at signing tables
- Include it in email newsletters
- Display it at events or appearances

Every scan is a chance for someone to discover your moments and order an official print. Make it easy for people to find your catalog.

---

## 7. Keep Your Profile Active

A profile with recent moments signals that you're an active creator. Even a single new release every few months keeps your catalog fresh and gives fans a reason to check back.

Your profile link and QR code are available in your Account under Marketing Tools.

---

## 8. Think Long Term

The creators who build the strongest connection with fans are the ones who treat their catalog like a body of work, not a one-time event.

Release thoughtfully. Enable prints on your best moments. Show up for your fans. Over time, a consistent catalog becomes easier for fans to follow, share, and collect.
