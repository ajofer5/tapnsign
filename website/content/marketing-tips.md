# Creator Marketing Tips

A TapnSign autograph is more than a signature — it's a verified digital collectible tied to your name, your image, and your reputation. How you build your catalog matters. Here's how to create lasting value for yourself and your collectors.

---

## 1. Start Small and Stay Consistent

Your first series doesn't need to be your biggest. Start with a small, focused release — even five to ten autographs — and deliver on it completely before launching another.

Collectors pay attention to creators who follow through. A small series with every autograph claimed is worth more than a large series that stalls halfway.

---

## 2. Use Series to Create Scarcity

Every autograph you create can be part of a named series with a defined size. Smaller series create more scarcity and tend to hold their value better over time.

- A series of 10 feels exclusive
- A series of 100 feels like a release
- An open edition feels unlimited

Think about what kind of collector you want to attract before you decide on a size.

---

## 3. Write a Series Name That Means Something

Your series name is permanent on every Certificate of Authenticity. Choose names that reflect a moment, a milestone, or a theme — not just a number.

Examples:
- "Opening Night" for your first release
- "Season 1" if you plan to release in chapters
- A city or event name if it was captured somewhere meaningful

Collectors remember names. Make yours worth remembering.

---

## 4. Price With Intention

Starting too high can slow your first release. Starting too low can make later releases hard to price.

A good starting approach: price your first series modestly, build a track record, and let demand drive prices up over time. Collectors who got in early at a lower price become your most loyal advocates.

---

## 5. Share Your QR Code Everywhere

Your TapnSign QR code links directly to your profile. Put it to work:

- Add it to your social media bio
- Print it on business cards or at signing tables
- Include it in email newsletters
- Display it at events or appearances

Every scan is a potential collector. Make it easy for people to find your digital catalog.

---

## 6. Keep Your Profile Active

A profile with recent autographs signals that you're an active creator. Even a single new release every few months keeps your catalog fresh and gives collectors a reason to check back.

Go-to-profile link and QR code are available in your Account under Marketing Tools.

---

## 7. Think Long Term

The creators who build the most value on TapnSign are the ones who treat their digital catalog like a body of work — not a one-time event.

Release thoughtfully. Price consistently. Show up for your collectors. The value of your autographs over time reflects the care you put into creating them.
